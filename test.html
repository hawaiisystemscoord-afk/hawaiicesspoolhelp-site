Frequently Asked Questions

How is this free for homeowners?

Engineers fund a small coordination fee. Your engineering price is the same as going direct.

Are you an engineer or a contractor?

No. We are a coordination service. Licensed civil engineers provide designs, and installation is performed by independent local contractors.

What do you do with my information?

We share your file only with the single licensed engineer and any installer you choose to contact. We never sell data.

Which areas do you cover?

Kona, Hilo, Waimea, Kohala, Puna, and Kaʻū on Hawaiʻi Island.
