About Us

Hawaii Cesspool Help is a local coordination service for homeowners. Our team has more than 40 years of combined experience in construction, drafting, and engineering in Hawaiʻi. We understand the building process and how to work with local professionals. We started this service to make it easier for homeowners to protect island water and meet the 2050 state mandate.

Frequently Asked Questions

How is this service free for homeowners?

Engineers pay a small coordination fee. Your design cost is the same as going direct.

Are you an engineer or a contractor?

No. We are a coordination service. Licensed civil engineers stamp your plans and we can refer qualified installers.

What do you do with my information?

We share your file only with the single licensed engineer who works on your project. We never sell data.

Which areas do you cover?

Kona, Hilo, Waimea, Kohala, Puna, and Kaʻū on Hawaiʻi Island.
