About Us

Hawaii Cesspool Help is a local coordination service for homeowners on Hawaiʻi Island. Our team has more than 50 years of combined experience in construction, drafting, and engineering. We live on island and work directly with local licensed engineers and trusted installers to move projects forward.

Each project protects our island’s water one conversion at a time.

Frequently asked questions

How is this free for homeowners?

We operate as part of a coordinated network funded by licensed engineers and installers allowing homeowners to access free project coordination without added cost or markup.

Are you an engineer or a contractor?

No, we are a coordination service. Licensed civil engineers provide designs and independent local contractors handle installation. We organize the steps and keep communication clear

What do you do with my information?

We share your file only with the single licensed engineer and any installer you choose. We never sell data.

Which areas do you cover?

Hilo, Puna, Kaʻū, Kona, Kohala, Hāmākua • All over the island of Hawaiʻi
