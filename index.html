Frequently Asked Questions

How is this service free for homeowners?

Our service is 100% free to you. We are paid a small coordination fee by our engineering partners for managing the project intake and paperwork. The cost for your septic design is the same as if you went to them directly.

Are you an engineer or a contractor?

No. We are a professional coordination service. We partner with licensed civil engineers who will stamp your plans and can refer you to qualified contractors for the installation.

What do you do with my information?

We only share your project details with the single licensed engineer who is matched to your project. We will never sell your information.

Which engineer will I be working with?

We partner with several licensed civil engineers on Hawaiʻi Island. We match you with an engineer based on your specific location and project requirements.
